Help Desk organize

A help box is a designated area on a website or application where users can access support or guidance. It often contains information, tips, FAQs, or links to additional resources. Help boxes improve user experience by offering quick solutions, clarifying doubts, and assisting users in navigating features effectively.

Online Seminar

An online seminar is a virtual event where experts present information or discuss specific topics to an online audience. Participants can interact through chat or Q&A sessions. Online seminars are popular for learning, networking, and professional development, offering convenience and flexibility to attend from anywhere with an internet connection.
